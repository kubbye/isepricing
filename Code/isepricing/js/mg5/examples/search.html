Menu G5 Examples: Embedding HTML form into the menu

yxScripts.com

 

Menu G5 supports Info Item. Info Item is just for display only and it doesn't have mouseover/mousedown effects. In this example, we use Info Items to hold a vote box and some search boxes. Just move your mouse over the Rate Me and Search to give them a try, and as long as your mouse is over the Info Item, it will stay visible to take your input.

Of course, as we are putting HTML codes in the addInfo() call, we will need to use the backslash (\) to escape the double quotes.

Update for IE/Mac: The search form in this example is not working in IE/Mac. Further testing needed.
